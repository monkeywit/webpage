About me

My name is Efrain and I am a mechatronic engineer (University of Pamplona, 2015), I received the degree of Mechatronics Master from the University of Brasilia (2018), and currently, I am PhD student at PPMEC, linked as researcher in the GIAI group. Since 2019, I have been developing teaching activities in the area of information systems and computing at UNIDESC.

My research interests are focused on parallel manipulator robots, additive manufacturing, standards for exchanging product and manufacturing data (STEP-NC, MTConnect), software for engineering applications, IoT, and mechatronics product development.
